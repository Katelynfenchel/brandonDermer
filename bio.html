Biography

Brandon Dermer is a writer/director currently in post-production for his creation WHAT WOULD DIPLO DO? with James Van Der Beek at Viceland. The show sold to Spike Jonze and Brandon directed the entire first season.

Brandon has directed for MTV, Comedy Central, Adult Swim and many others. His music videos have been featured at SXSW. MTV's VMAs, Berlin Music Video Film Festival, Vimeo Staff Pick, Huffington Post, Rolling Stone Magazine, The Front Page of Reddit, AV Club, Vulture and much more capturing millions of views online.
